Special Events

Arts and Crafts Station

Timing: 3:00 PM - 3:30 PM (30 minutes)

  • Set up a colorful table with supplies like paper crowns, beads for bracelets, markers, stickers, and glitter glue.
  • Each child can make their own unique craft item, such as a crown, bracelet, or mask.
  • At the end, showcase their creations in a mini art gallery or let them wear/use their crafts.
  • This activity encourages creativity and ensures everyone has a personalized keepsake.

Treasure Hunt

Timing: 3:45 PM - 4:15 PM (30 minutes)

  • Prepare themed clues and hide small treasures (stickers, candies, small toys) around the venue.
  • Divide the children into small teams and hand them their first clue to start the hunt.
  • The final treasure could be a chest filled with goodies that everyone shares.
  • This activity promotes teamwork, excitement, and adventure.

Cupcake Decoration

Timing: 4:30 PM - 5:00 PM (30 minutes)

  • Provide plain cupcakes along with bowls of frosting (different colors), sprinkles, edible decorations, and tiny candies.
  • Provide aprons and chef hats for the kids to wear during the activity, adding a touch of fun and making them feel like little bakers.
  • Each child decorates their cupcake as they like.
  • Once done, the kids can enjoy their delicious creations or take them home in a cute box.
  • This is a fun and tasty activity that engages their creativity and taste buds.
